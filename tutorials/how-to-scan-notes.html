How to scan Notes and create PDF on your Phone

Step 1: Download Adobe Scan

Install Adobe Scan PDF Scanner from Google Play Store

Step 2: Add your account

Open Adobe Scan and Sign in with your Account. We are using Google Sign In for this tutorial. Tap on Sign in with Google

Step 3: Select your account

Select your preferred account

Step 4: Allow Camera Permission

Adobe Scan requires Camera permission in order to scan your document. Allow camera permission to the App

Step 5: Scan your document

Now place your paper on a flat surface and capture the picture from the Top

Step 5: Crop Document

After capturing the picture Adobe scan will ask you to adjust the crop if required. Then tap on Continue

Step 6: Finish Scan

After capturing all the pages, now you can tap on the little image shown at bottom right corner to access all your scanned pages

Step 7: Save PDF

Now tap on pencil icon to rename the file if required. Then tap on Save PDF option on the top right corner

Step 7:Share PDF

Now tap on Share option to share your PDF

Tap on Share a copy option to share the PDF file
